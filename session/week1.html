Instructor: Vaishnavy Shanmuganathan

Session Overview

This session introduced the key components of employability skills, focusing on the technical, soft, and interpersonal skills required to succeed in the modern workforce. The importance of aligning personal values and career goals with employer expectations was also discussed.

Key Topics Covered

  • Soft Skills for Career Success - Key communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills that enhance employability.
  • Technical Skills in the Workplace - The importance of relevant technical skills in various industries.
  • Values, Beliefs, and Attitudes - How personal values, beliefs, and attitudes affect professional development and career growth.
  • Transferable Skills - Skills that can be applied across various roles and industries.

Key Takeaways

  • Develop Soft Skills - Focus on improving communication, problem-solving, and interpersonal relationships.
  • Build Technical Proficiency - Gain proficiency in industry-relevant technologies and tools.
  • Align with Employer Needs - Understanding the specific skills employers value and aligning your growth plan accordingly.

Session Details

  • Focus on professional communication and problem-solving in real-world scenarios.
  • Understanding the role of personal attitudes and behaviors in a professional setting.
  • Introduction to transferable skills that enhance employability in different fields.

Topic Summary

This session emphasized the development of both soft and technical skills, highlighting the need for continuous learning, effective communication, and alignment with employer expectations. The role of personal values and attitudes in professional growth was also discussed.

What Was Taught

We covered the critical skills needed for employability, including communication, technical skills, teamwork, and time management. We also discussed the significance of personal attributes such as values, beliefs, and attitudes in shaping one's career trajectory.

What Was Learned

The session emphasized the need to develop both technical and soft skills for career success. Aligning one's personal values and skills with the needs of the job market is essential. We also completed a task identifying key technical, soft, and transferable skills.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

I plan to further develop my soft skills in areas such as communication and teamwork, while continuing to enhance my technical skills, ensuring I align with the requirements of future employers in the IT sector.
