Entrepreneurship & Career Growth

Week 3

Conducted By: Gajarthan Thevarajah, Founder & Director Bohar Solutions

Date: 02 August 2025

Session Overview

We had the amazing opportunity to learn from Mr. Gajarthan Thevarajah, an inspiring entrepreneur and industry expert, during our Enterprise Skills Development (ESD) session for Year 2 Semester 2 at SLIIT Northern University. The session focused on essential entrepreneurial skills, career growth, and building a mindset for success.

Key Takeaways

  • Importance of Starting Without Being an Expert - Learning to take the first step, even without full expertise.
  • Leveraging Your Second Year as a Secret Advantage - How to use your current academic journey to build a foundation for the future.
  • Building Step by Step to Reach Your Goals - The value of incremental progress in achieving entrepreneurial success.
  • Owning Your Campus Journey as Your Career Story - Making your university experience a key part of your professional narrative.
  • Mindset Shift - “The only difference between you and the people you admire is that they started – now it’s your turn.”

Key Takeaways

  • Start Without Waiting for Perfection - Emphasizing action over perfection in the entrepreneurial journey.
  • Strategize for Career Growth - Understanding how small decisions can build toward a larger vision.
  • Adopting an Entrepreneurial Mindset - Embracing a mindset shift for long-term success in any career path.

Session Details

  • Learning the importance of starting a business without being an expert.
  • Understanding how to leverage academic years for career advantage.
  • Insights into building your career step by step.
  • Discussion on owning your campus journey as your career story.
  • Mindset shift that encourages taking the first step, no matter where you are.

Topic Summary

This session offered valuable insights on entrepreneurship and career growth. We discussed practical advice for overcoming self-doubt and taking action toward goals, while also understanding how to make the most of our academic years to build a strong career foundation.

What Was Taught

We learned the importance of starting without being an expert and the power of leveraging our current academic stage. The session also emphasized building careers step by step and adopting the right mindset for entrepreneurial success.

What Was Learned

The session focused on the significance of starting where we are, making progress, and adopting an entrepreneurial mindset for future career growth. It encouraged us to take the leap, no matter where we stand in our journey.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

I plan to implement the insights gained about starting early, leveraging my current position, and building my career step by step. I will also work on shifting my mindset to take consistent action towards my professional goals.
